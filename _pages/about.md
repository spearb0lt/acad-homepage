Hi, I am Shubhro Dev, a final-year Computer Science and Engineering undergraduate at the [Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Petroleum Technology (RGIPT)](https://www.rgipt.ac.in/), an Institute of National Importance established along the lines of the IITs. I work at the intersection of large language models, multimodal deep learning, federated learning and time-series analysis, and I care most about building models that are not just accurate but also verifiable and privacy preserving.

I am currently a Technical Research Intern with the Global DAI R (SDE-IN) team at [Siemens](https://www.siemens.com/), researching the validation and verification of outputs generated by LLMs. Previously I worked as a research assistant and research intern at the VANET Lab (IIT Jodhpur), VCA Lab (IIT BHU Varanasi) and CMATER Lab (Jadavpur University), and as a data science / machine learning intern at Core-AI Solutions and Adani Power, across predictive maintenance, medical imaging and multimodal knowledge retrieval. I also placed at Global Rank 10 in the Meta Hacker Cup 2025 (AI Track).
